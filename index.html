About us
Your neighborhood Chinese restaurant since 2000. Our food is of the best quality. We handroll spring rolls and dumplings. We always keep our food with the best taste and always kept fresh. We have the newest POS system which keeps record of customers’ order history including customer’s preferences. You can ask cashier what you ordered last time. We don’t use any third-party delivery company or websites. We provide free delivery within 5 miles. Delivery fee may apply beyond this point.
